What You'll Learn

Comprehensive Yoga Curriculum

Hatha Yoga Practice

Master traditional Hatha yoga postures, alignment, and breathing techniques. Our rishikesh yoga training emphasizes classical methods passed down through generations of gurus.

Ashtanga Vinyasa

Learn the primary and intermediate series of Ashtanga yoga with proper vinyasa count, bandhas, and drishti. This dynamic practice builds strength, flexibility, and inner focus.

Yoga Philosophy

Study Patanjali's Yoga Sutras, Bhagavad Gita, Hatha Yoga Pradipika, and ancient scriptures. Understand the eight limbs of yoga and apply them to modern teaching and daily life.

Pranayama & Meditation

Practice various pranayama techniques including Nadi Shodhana, Kapalabhati, and Bhastrika. Learn meditation methods from mantra to vipassana at our yoga and meditation center in Rishikesh.

Anatomy & Physiology

Understand the physical body through modern anatomy and the subtle body through chakras, nadis, and koshas. This knowledge helps you teach safely and effectively to diverse students.

Ayurveda & Nutrition

Explore Ayurvedic principles, doshas, and sattvic diet. Learn how nutrition supports yoga practice and how to guide students toward balanced living according to their unique constitution.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the yoga teacher training course fees in Rishikesh?

Our yoga teacher training course fees in Rishikesh include accommodation, three daily sattvic meals, all training sessions, course materials, Yoga Alliance certification, weekend excursions, and airport pickup. There are no hidden charges throughout your stay at our yoga school in Rishikesh.

Do I need prior yoga experience to join?

No prior experience is required for our 200 hour yoga teacher training in Rishikesh. Beginners are welcome. However, for the 300 hour advanced program, we recommend at least one year of regular practice. Our teachers adapt instructions to every level at this best yoga center in Rishikesh.

Is the certification recognized worldwide?

Yes. Our rishikesh yoga teacher training center is registered with Yoga Alliance USA. Upon completion, you receive a globally recognized RYT certification allowing you to teach yoga anywhere in the world. We are among the top yoga schools in Rishikesh with this accreditation.

What styles of yoga are taught?

We teach traditional Hatha, Ashtanga Vinyasa, Vinyasa Flow, Iyengar alignment, and Kundalini yoga. Students also learn pranayama, meditation, and yoga philosophy. This diverse curriculum makes us the best yoga teacher training school in Rishikesh India for holistic learning.

How do I reach Rishikesh from the airport?

The nearest airport is Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun, about 45 minutes from our yoga training center in Rishikesh. We provide complimentary airport pickup for all enrolled students. Delhi airport is also an option, with a 6-hour drive or train ride to learn yoga in Rishikesh.
